Magic-symbol-directive

An angularJS directive to consume and display Magic: the Gathering mana costs from the mtgJSON format.

An easy way to display mtgJSON!

The project mtgJSON, is a Magic: the Gathering card datasource that is easily read in javascript! Magic-symbol-directive is an AngularJS directive that helps us process this:

How to use

Include the magic-symbol-directive dependency on your angular module! 
	
var app = angular.module("yourapp", ['magicSymbolDirective']);

After that, it is a matter of referencing a card object in your controller's scope and then wire it to either the casting-cost property of the target element for parsing casting cost: 

	
<p>Text: <span casting-cost="card.manaCost"></span></p>

or the card-text property for parsing card text: 

	
<p>Text: <span card-text="card.text"></span></p>
